Welcome to Orbit Hotel, your home away from home in Dire Dawa. We give you the best of Ethiopia and Africa: good food and premium accomodation. We are located just 2 minutes from the airport. So whether you're staying with us or dining in, we honor the communal spirit of Ethiopian and African hospitality culture, tending to you with love and care. From our aromatic coffee ceremony to the bold flavors of our wots and tibs, and from comfortable overnight stays to memorable meals, every experience celebrates heritage, warmth, and togetherness.
